Traditional learning platforms give everyone the same content. Skill-Tango does things differently.

## The Adaptive Assessment

Before you start any course, our AI gives you a short baseline assessment. This isn't a test you can fail — it's how we understand exactly where you are right now. Whether you're a complete beginner or have years of experience, we meet you where you are.

## AI-Generated Curricula

Based on your assessment results, our AI generates a completely custom curriculum. This includes:

- **Chapter structure** tailored to your current knowledge gaps
- **Lesson depth** that matches your learning pace
- **Exercise types** suited to your level
- **Multi-modal content** — text, audio narration, and visual aids

## Continuous Adaptation

As you complete lessons and quizzes, the system learns what works for you. Struggling with a concept? We'll generate additional explanations. Breezing through? We'll accelerate.

## The Tech Behind It

We use NVIDIA NIMs to power our AI pipeline — from assessment generation to curriculum creation to lesson content. Each step uses carefully crafted prompts that our admin team can update in real-time via our CMS.
